## About (2,600 char limit)

> After 22 years across Fortune 500 systems integrators, Tier-1 Indian IT services firms, and global consulting practices, I started AICloudStrategist — a focused cloud cost consultancy for Indian SMBs and mid-market teams.
>
> **The problem I keep seeing:**
>
> Companies that moved to AWS, Azure, or GCP during 2020–2022 under growth pressure. The architecture made sense then. Three years of unreviewed auto-scaling, storage tiers, and provisioning later — 15 to 30 percent of the bill is avoidable. It does not appear as a flagged line item. It compounds quietly.
>
> Most of the cost-optimisation work I did inside large organisations saved Fortune 500 clients ₹30–50L a year. That senior-architect attention rarely reaches the Indian mid-market founder running a ₹5–20L/month cloud bill. They get a vendor call and a 20-page proposal that no one acts on.
>
> **What AICloudStrategist does differently:**
>
> → One practitioner, direct engagement. No junior teams doing the analysis, no offshore hand-offs.
> → 4-week fixed scope. 20–30% verified reduction, or you don't pay the implementation fee.
> → Open-source tooling (OpenCost, Infracost, OpenTelemetry). No licence lock-in, full audit trail.
> → INR-first pricing. Transparent gain-share on verified savings.
>
> **Typical findings in a first review:**
>
> • Reserved Instance coverage below 30% on stable workloads (should be 70%+)
> • S3 / Blob storage in hot tiers holding data untouched for 12+ months
> • GKE / EKS node pools sized for peak QPS that hits twice a quarter
> • Cross-region egress for workloads that could run in source region
> • Orphaned EBS volumes, unattached disks, and stale snapshots
>
> **Recent engagements (anonymised):**
>
> D2C e-commerce, AWS ~₹35L/month → ₹9.4L/month savings verified in 5 weeks
> B2B SaaS, multi-region GCP ~₹18L/month → 27% reduction sustained 8+ months
>
> **If your cloud bill has grown faster than your business,** I offer a free 30-minute Cloud Cost Health Check — a structured look at your setup, no sales process, written summary same day.
>
