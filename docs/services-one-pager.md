## Where to start

**First time engaging us** — begin with the free 30-minute Cloud Cost Health Check. You share a 7-day Cost and Usage Report sample. You receive a two-page written summary by end of the same day, covering the top three cost leaks we would attack first and the recoverable INR against each. No sales process. No follow-up sequence.

**Cost is the pain point** — FinOps QuickStart (Service 01). The lead service. Most engagements start here.

**Architecture or governance is the pain point** — Cloud Architecture Review (Service 07). Senior-architect-grade assessment ahead of a funding round, scale event, or post-incident review.

**AI / GPU spend growing uncontrollably** — AI Architecture Review (Service 08). Cost-per-inference visibility and the build-vs-buy framework most AI teams lack.

**Deployments are slow, risky, or manual** — DevOps & Platform Engineering (Service 09). DORA-metric-measured engagement.

**You have a diagnostic already (ours or someone else's)** — Implementation Sprint (Service 04). We execute the backlog.
