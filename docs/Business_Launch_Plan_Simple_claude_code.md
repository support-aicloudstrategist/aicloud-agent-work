## The legal stuff (India)

You need to set up:

1. **Sole Proprietorship** — the simplest type of business in India. No company registration needed.
2. **Udyam (MSME)** — free, online, 30 minutes
3. **Delhi Shops Act** — free, online, 1–2 days
4. **Bank account** for business
5. **GST registration** — free, 7–10 days. Companies want this.
6. **LUT** — lets you bill foreign clients without GST

**Tax:** Use **Section 44ADA** — you only pay tax on 50% of what you earn (Indian government scheme for consultants). Limit: ₹75 lakh/year.

**Customer payments:** They'll cut 10% TDS (tax) from what they pay you. You get it back when filing tax returns.

**DPDP (data privacy law):** Don't worry yet — main rules don't kick in until **May 2027**. You have time.

## The AI agents — how they help

You message Telegram. Behind the scenes:

```
You type something on Telegram
       ↓
Bot decides: simple or complex?
       ↓
   Simple → Bot answers itself (cheap)
   Complex → Bot asks Claude Max to do it (free, you already pay)
       ↓
Bot sends you the result on Telegram
```

**Plus 3 helper workflows:**
- **LeadScout** — finds potential customers on LinkedIn, scores them
- **ContentDraft** — writes LinkedIn posts and outreach messages — **YOU approve before they go out**
- **InvoiceBot** — sends invoices automatically when you finish work

**Big rule:** AI never sends a message to anyone without your "yes" first. This protects your reputation.

## The 30-day plan to get your first customer

### Week 1 — Setup (you do paperwork, I build tech)
- You: Register Udyam, GST, bank account
- Me: Build website, set up email, build the AI agents
- Both: Write 2 free guides ("AWS cost cutting checklist", "Cloud maturity self-assessment")

### Week 2 — Reach out
- **Personal WhatsApp/voice notes to 30 ex-colleagues** (Tech Mahindra, IBM, HCL, etc.) — "Hey, looking for CTOs with high cloud bills. Anyone you can introduce me to?"
- **Send 20 LinkedIn connection requests per day** to CTOs (manually, with personal note)
- **Post on LinkedIn** 2 times per week (AI writes draft, you edit, you post)

### Week 3 — Free check-ups
- Do **4–6 free 30-minute cloud check-ups** for interested people
- Send each one a 2-page report (AI helps draft, you finalize in 30 min)
- Follow up: "I found ₹X waste. Want me to fix it for ₹75K?"
- Send **2–3 paid proposals**

### Week 4 — Close the deal
- Sign first customer
- Take 50% advance, 50% on completion
- Start the 2-week project
