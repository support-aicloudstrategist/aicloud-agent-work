# LinkedIn Banner — Design Brief

**Specs:** 1584 × 396 px (LinkedIn standard)
**Safe area:** Stay within 1350 × 220 px centred — LinkedIn crops edges on mobile and the profile photo covers the bottom-left corner (~220 × 220 px bubble)
**Format:** PNG or JPG under 8 MB

---

## Design Direction

**Mood:** Calm, confident, senior. Not startup-y. Not flashy. Think McKinsey or Duckbill Group — not a SaaS landing page.

**Colour palette (pick one):**

| Option | Primary | Accent | Text |
|---|---|---|---|
| **A — Deep Navy** (recommended) | `#0B2545` | `#FCA311` (warm amber) | `#FFFFFF` |
| **B — Forest Ink** | `#0F3D3E` | `#E0C097` (sand) | `#FFFFFF` |
| **C — Charcoal Cloud** | `#1C1C1E` | `#5AC8FA` (cool blue) | `#FFFFFF` |

**Typography:**
- Primary: **Inter Bold** or **Source Sans Pro Bold** (clean, senior, modern)
- Secondary: **Inter Regular**
- Avoid: Comic Sans, Montserrat ExtraBold, any script/handwriting font

**Layout — centre-right composition:**

```
┌──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                                                          │
│                            AICloudStrategist             │
│                            Founder-led. Enterprise-      │
│                            reviewed.                     │
│                                                          │
│                            Cut your cloud bill 20–30%    │
│                            in four weeks.                │
│                            FinOps · Cloud & AI Architecture │
│                            DevOps · Security             │
│                                                          │
│                            aicloudstrategist.com         │
│  [photo] ←  profile photo overlay zone                   │
└──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

Text sits in the right 2/3. The left 1/3 is clear so the profile photo bubble doesn't collide with text.

## Text Content

**Line 1 (largest, 72pt):**
> AICloudStrategist

**Line 2 (36pt, amber/accent colour):**
> Founder-led. Enterprise-reviewed.

**Line 3 (28pt, white, bold):**
> Cut your cloud bill 20–30% in four weeks.

**Line 4 (20pt, white, regular, spaced):**
> FinOps · Cloud & AI Architecture · DevOps · Security

**Line 5 (16pt, amber/accent, bottom-right):**
> aicloudstrategist.com

---

## Optional Decorative Element

A single clean visual — pick one, keep it subtle:

- A thin horizontal rule between lines 3 and 4
- A small cloud-silhouette icon (flat, single-colour) on the far right
- Three small vertical markers (▌ ▌ ▌) representing the three pillars (FinOps, DevOps, Security)

**No stock photos of servers.** No "networking" graphics. No gradient backgrounds with glowing lines. No people sitting at laptops.

---

## What Not to Do

- Do not use the word "AI" in a large, standalone way (it reads generic in 2026)
- Do not use generic cloud imagery (fluffy clouds, server racks, glowing networks)
- Do not claim "trusted by Fortune 500" or similar — untrue for AICloudStrategist as a practice
- Do not include Anushka's photo in the banner — profile photo overlays the banner already
- Do not use more than two colours + white
- Do not use italic body text (reads as a pull-quote, not a tagline)

---

## Accept Criteria

A good banner passes these three tests:

1. **3-second test:** someone scrolling LinkedIn can read "AICloudStrategist" + the tagline in under 3 seconds
2. **Greyscale test:** converted to black-and-white, the hierarchy still works
3. **Mobile crop test:** at 1200×230 cropped width, the core text is still intact
