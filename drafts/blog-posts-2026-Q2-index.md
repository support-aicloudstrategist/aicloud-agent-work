**Host on aicloudstrategist.com/blog. Link from LinkedIn, don't publish natively.**

Every CTA in this batch points to aicloudstrategist.com — the checklist PDFs, the self-assessment, book.html. Publishing natively on LinkedIn sends readers into LinkedIn's ecosystem and loses the conversion path. Hosting on your own domain builds domain authority, keeps analytics clean, and routes every lead through your funnel. LinkedIn posts (not articles) with a strong hook and "link in comments" get comparable organic reach to native articles and cost nothing in SEO equity.

Reserve LinkedIn native articles for content with no external CTA — thought leadership or opinion pieces. These five posts are lead-gen assets; treat them that way.
