# LinkedIn Post — FinOps Maturity in 10 Minutes

You don't need a consultant to tell you where you stand on FinOps maturity. You need 10 minutes and honest answers to 5 questions.

**Do this right now:**

1. Can you attribute >80% of your cloud spend to a team or product? *(Yes/No)*
2. Do you review your Reserved Instance / Savings Plan coverage weekly? *(Yes/No)*
3. Is there a named person — not a committee — accountable for the monthly cloud bill? *(Yes/No)*
4. Do your engineers see their team's cloud costs in their daily workflow? *(Yes/No)*
5. Have you acted on a cost anomaly alert in the last 30 days — not just acknowledged it? *(Yes/No)*

**Score:**
- 5/5 → Optimising phase. You're in the top 15% of Indian mid-market companies.
- 3-4/5 → Running phase. You have the basics but leaking 15-25% unnecessarily.
- 0-2/5 → Crawl phase. Expect 30%+ avoidable spend — every month.

Most teams I talk to score 1 or 2.

It's not a skills problem. It's a process and visibility problem — both fixable in 4 weeks.
