Case 1 — D2C e-commerce, AWS, ~₹35L/month spend

Findings: 41% of compute on idle instances after a holiday-traffic-driven scale-up that was never reversed. Storage tier mismatch on S3 (Standard for cold archives). EBS volumes orphaned after EC2 termination.

Action: Right-sized 60% of EC2 fleet, lifecycle policies on S3, cleanup automation for orphaned EBS, RI + SP coverage strategy.

Outcome: Verified ₹9.4L/month savings in 5 weeks. Implemented OpenCost + tagging baseline so engineering team owns ongoing cost.

Case 2 — B2B SaaS, multi-region GCP, ~₹18L/month spend

Findings: GKE node pools provisioned for peak QPS that hit only twice a quarter; cross-region egress for analytics queries that could run in source region; persistent disks unattached for 90+ days.

Action: Cluster autoscaler tuning, regional analytics warehouse, disk reclamation cron, committed-use discounts.

Outcome: 27% cloud-bill reduction sustained for 8+ months. Engineering KPI now includes cost-per-tenant.
