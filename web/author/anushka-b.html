What I do

I run AICloudStrategist — a solo-founded cloud cost consultancy focused on Indian SMBs and mid-market companies running on AWS, Azure, or GCP. We help teams cut cloud bills 20–30% in about four weeks through structured FinOps engagements, open-source tooling, and a senior-architect oversight layer that carries 22+ years of Fortune 500 cloud-economics weight.

Every engagement is delivered personally by me, reviewed by a senior architect before anything goes to the client. Fixed fees, no hourly billing, optional gain-share on verified savings. The combination small consultancies skip and big firms cannot commercially match.
