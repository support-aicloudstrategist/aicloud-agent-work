Frequently asked

Why only three?

Delivery capacity. Founder-led means Anushka is the person doing the work — not a junior analyst. Three concurrent QuickStart engagements is the realistic maximum we can run at founder-led quality through May. Beyond three, we are back to standard pricing.

What if the engagement does not surface enough savings to be worth ₹40,000?

In every mid-market audit we have done, we have surfaced at least 15% in addressable monthly spend. On a ₹8 lakh/month AWS account that is ₹14–₹20 lakh/year in recoverable cost — the ₹40K engagement pays for itself inside the first month of implementation.

If for some reason we genuinely cannot surface that — we will tell you honestly during Week 1 and pivot the engagement to an architecture review or reliability focus at no additional cost. You will never pay ₹40K for an engagement that delivers nothing.

How does the named case study work?

At engagement close, we write up a one-page case study covering: your company at a high level (industry, rough spend band, what you run), the three most impactful findings, the ₹ recovered (or projected). You see the draft before anything is published. You can redact anything commercially sensitive. You can say no at any point before publication. If you say no post-draft, we still honour the ₹40K pricing — we just move on.

Do you have existing case studies we can see?

Our existing engagements to date have asked to stay anonymous. You will see the pattern and the numbers on aicloudstrategist.com/proof.html but not the company names. That is exactly why named case study rights are worth the ₹35K discount to us — a public logo compounds trust in a way anonymised figures cannot.

Is this for AWS only?

AWS, Azure, GCP, or multi-cloud. Our methodology is cloud-agnostic. The tooling (Infracost, OpenCost, FOCUS exports) works across all three.

What if we need a different service (architecture review, security, DevOps)?

The first-customer discount applies to the QuickStart only — that is our lead service and the one we can deliver at first-customer volume. For Architecture Review, Security Posture Review, or a DevOps engagement, we are happy to talk and scope separately, but those are priced at standard rates.

GST and billing?
