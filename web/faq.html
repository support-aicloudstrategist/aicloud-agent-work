The questions CTOs and CFOs actually ask before booking a call. If yours is not here, email support@aicloudstrategist.com and we will answer within the same business day.

What does a FinOps QuickStart actually deliver?

A ranked savings backlog with verified INR figures against every line item, a cost-allocation tagging baseline, and a 90-day implementation plan with named owners. Two-week engagement, fixed price ₹75,000–₹1,50,000 depending on account complexity. See full service catalogue.

What is the Cloud Cost Health Check and is it really free?

Yes, genuinely free. A 30-minute structured call, plus a two-page written summary delivered by end of the same business day — covering the top three leaks we see in your data and the recoverable INR against each. No sales follow-up sequence. You keep the summary regardless of whether you engage us further.

How do you actually save 20–30% without re-architecture?

By closing structural pricing gaps that already exist in the account — not by redesigning anything. Reserved Instance and Savings Plan coverage below 40%, rightsizing oversized compute that nobody reviewed after migration, lifecycle policies on cold storage, reclaiming orphaned volumes and stale snapshots, fixing commitment mismatches from 2-year-old purchases. None of these require code or architecture changes. They require a decision that never got made.

Do you sign an NDA before seeing our data?

Yes. A mutual NDA is signed before any client billing data, architecture diagrams, or credentials are shared. Our Master Services Agreement embeds DPDP Act 2023 readiness clauses. The NDA can be executed via Aadhaar eSign in under ten minutes.

How is gain-share structured?

On cost-focused engagements, a gain-share model is available as an alternative or supplement to the fixed fee. We agree a verified-savings floor. Any monthly savings above the floor shares a percentage with AICloudStrategist for an agreed measurement window (typically 3–6 months post-implementation), subject to a cap. If verified savings do not cross the floor, no gain-share is due — only the fixed fee applies. Structure is always documented in the SoW before kickoff, never negotiated mid-engagement.

Do you have access to our production infrastructure during an engagement?

For diagnostic engagements (FinOps QuickStart, Architecture Review, Security Posture Review) we request read-only IAM access scoped to the accounts in review. No write access. For implementation engagements, access scope is agreed workstream-by-workstream and documented in the SoW — minimum required, for the minimum duration required. Credentials are rotated at engagement end and we provide written confirmation of access removal.

Who actually does the work — the founder or a junior team?

Founder-led. The person you meet in the Health Check is the engineer writing the Terraform, running the analysis, and authoring the written deliverable you will read. Every finding also passes through a senior-architect review layer — 22+ years of Fortune 500 systems-integrator and global-consulting experience — before it reaches your inbox. No junior analysts. No offshore hand-offs. No consulting-partner-sells-while-associate-delivers model.

Is our cloud spend too small to engage you?

If your monthly cloud bill is under ₹2L, our fee structure does not make commercial sense — the savings ceiling is too low to justify the engagement. We will tell you that honestly in the Health Check and point you to our free resources (AWS Cost Checklist, Azure Cost Checklist, FinOps Maturity Self-Assessment) so you can execute the work yourself.

What if we already bought CloudHealth or Flexera?

Tools surface data. Someone still has to act on the data. Most teams we work with already have one of these dashboards installed — nobody has the 30 hours per month to interpret it, build the RI purchase models, and drive the execution. That is the gap our retainer and QuickStart services close. We are happy to integrate with your existing tooling rather than displace it; many engagements begin with a QuickStart that specifically acts on findings the client's existing tool has been flagging for months.

Can you help if we have never done a cost review before?

Those are exactly the accounts where our engagements deliver the largest recoverable spend — 20–30% reductions in the first review are not unusual when the baseline has never been formally examined. The Health Check is designed specifically for teams in this situation.
