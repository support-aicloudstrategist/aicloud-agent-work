What we will cover

The 5 cost patterns I see in nearly every mid-market audit

  1. Reserved Instance coverage below 30% — the belief-vs-data gap that quietly costs ₹3-8L/month on a typical ₹10L account
  2. Cross-region egress compounding silently — one Terraform region flag 3x-ing a bill over six months
  3. Orphaned EBS + unattached volumes — the most common overlooked line item, typically ₹3-5L/year of pure waste
  4. Tagging coverage gaps — 20-35% of spend sitting in untagged resources, preventing chargeback
  5. GPU instance over-provisioning — AWS raised GPU prices 15% in January 2026 with permanent lock-in in April; every team using AI workloads needs to review this
