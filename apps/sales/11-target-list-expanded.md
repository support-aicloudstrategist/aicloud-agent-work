## How to use this expanded list

**Immediate action (Day 3-4):**
1. Pick 10 from Tier 1A (AI infra — hottest wedge with GPU price hike)
2. For each, find: CTO/VP Eng/Head of Platform name via website /about, /team, /careers
3. Guess email using the pattern cheat sheet in `08-target-company-list.md`
4. Personalize first line with: recent funding / GPU announcement / NVIDIA partnership / Twitter post / engineering blog post
5. Send cold email #1 from `04-cold-email-sequence.md`

**Day 5-6:**
6. Move to Tier 1B SaaS Series A-B — these close faster than enterprise
7. Apollo free tier (25 lookups/month) for the specific CTO emails where guesses bounce
8. Hunter.io free (25 searches/month) for the common patterns per domain

**Day 7-10:**
9. Tier 2 Fintech — longer sales cycles but bigger budgets
10. Tier 4 B2B enterprise — slower but if ANY lands, it's a major reference

**Skip for now:**
- Tier 5 (public companies, procurement hell)
- Tier 3 consumer tech (long cycles, complex procurement)

**Realistic conversion math on 100 targets:**
- Send 100 Email 1s over 10 days (10/day)
- Email 2 follow-up on Day 4 after Email 1 → ~25 re-sends per week (if no reply)
- Email 3 value-only on Day 10 → ~15 re-sends
- Total email sends over sprint: ~140 individual emails
- Expected replies (8-15% on good targeting): 8-15
- Expected discovery calls: 4-8
- Expected proposals: 2-4
- Expected closes at 20-40% proposal-to-close: **1-2 signed**

That math works. One signed deal from cold email by day 20 is realistic.
