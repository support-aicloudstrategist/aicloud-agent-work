# Partnership Outreach Emails — CA Firms + MSPs + Dev Agencies

**Purpose:** Build a referral pipeline for month 2-3 while executing direct outreach in month 1.

**Why this works:** CA firms see every cloud bill during year-end audit. MSPs deploy AWS but lack FinOps depth. Boutique dev agencies ship to AWS but don't optimize cost. Every one of these has clients who need what we sell — and none of them compete with us directly.

**Referral structure we're offering:**
- 10% of first-engagement fee (₹4K on a ₹40K first-customer deal; ₹7.5K on a standard QuickStart; scales up)
- 5% of gain-share for 12 months (real upside if client converts to retainer)
- Partner does zero work — we sign our own SOW, deliver, invoice directly
- Transparent ledger: monthly statement of pipeline + paid + outstanding
